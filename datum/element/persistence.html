/datum/element/persistence

Simple object persistence elements For now, it just attachs and detaches and all objects with it are recorded to disk based on map and stuff at round end Can expand later for more functionality

WARNING: All atoms recorded are stored to turf! This does not yet support things like loading into inventories. This is mostly for on-floor objects.

Vars

objectsList of persistent atoms. Associated list to their GUID

Procs

DeserializeAndInstantiateAllInstantiates all objects from a data list and attaches to them.
SerializeAllSerializes all objects into a list for json encode or anything else needed. Includes data the datum includes in serialize_list(), the GUID of the datum's entry, and its turf location.
